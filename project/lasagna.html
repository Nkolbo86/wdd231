Homemade Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic until browned.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, salt, pepper, and basil. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella.
  7. Repeat layers and top with Parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake for 35-40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  9. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
