Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour and baking soda.
  3. In another bowl, beat butter, sugars, salt, and vanilla until creamy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture, then stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Let cool and enjoy!
