Grandma's Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 6-8 fresh apples, peeled and sliced
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare the pie crust by mixing flour and butter until crumbly.
  3. Roll out the dough and place it in a pie dish.
  4. Mix apples, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice in a bowl.
  5. Pour the apple mixture into the crust.
  6. Cover with the second layer of crust and crimp the edges.
  7. Brush with egg wash and cut slits in the top for venting.
  8. Bake for 45-50 minutes until golden brown.
  9. Let cool before serving.
