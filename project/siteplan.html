Family Recipe Site - Site Plan

Site Name

Family Recipe Collection

This site is dedicated to preserving and sharing our family's favorite recipes for future generations.

Site Purpose

The site allows family members to share, browse, and store recipes, creating a digital family cookbook. Users can submit new recipes, view existing ones, and save their favorites.

Scenarios

  • How can I add my grandmother's famous apple pie recipe to the collection?
  • Where can I find a list of our family's favorite holiday dishes?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #A52A2A (Headings, Navigation)
  • Secondary Color: #EAC67A (Footer, Highlights)
  • Accent 1: #F4A261 (Borders, Buttons)
  • Accent 2: #FAF3DD (Background)

Typography

  • Heading Font: Montserrat (used for page titles and section headings)
  • Body Font: Open Sans (used for paragraph text and descriptions)
