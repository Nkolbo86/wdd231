Family Recipe Sharing Website – Site Plan

Site Name

Family Recipe Sharing
This name reflects the goal of preserving and sharing traditional family recipes with loved ones. It emphasizes warmth, connection, and legacy through food.

Site Purpose

The website allows users to browse cherished family recipes and submit their own. It preserves traditions by keeping recipes in one place and makes it easy for family members to view or add to them. The site also includes a dynamic recipe gallery, submission form, and modal instructions for selected dishes.

Scenarios

  • How can I share my own recipe with the family?
  • Where can I browse classic recipes like Grandma’s Apple Pie or Dad’s Lasagna?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Deep Blue (#001F3F) – used for headers, buttons, and accents.
  • Accent Color: Red (#cc0000) – used for call-to-action buttons and highlights.
  • Background: White (#ffffff) – main page background.
  • Text: Black (#000000) – primary body text for readability.

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Merriweather', serif – used for all headings.
  • Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif – used for paragraphs and lists.
